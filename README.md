# Netflix-Data-Analysis

This project focused on analyzing Netflix's vast content library, aiming to answer several key questions:

- What are the most popular genres on Netflix?
-How is content distributed by duration, including short films, long movies, and TV shows?
- What are the growth trends of Netflix's catalog over the years?
- How many movies are available on Netflix?
- What are the average rating scores across different genres?
- What is the average length of movies vs. shows?
- How is content split between movies and TV shows?
